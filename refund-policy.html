Refund Policy

Effective Date: 28/11/2024

Last Updated: 28/11/2024

At Inferno Commit to Be Fit Gym, we strive to provide high-quality services and facilities to our members. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued and the process for requesting them.

1. Membership Fees

1.1. Non-Refundable Payments:

Membership fees are generally non-refundable once processed, except as outlined in this policy or required by law.

1.2. Exceptions:

Refunds for membership fees may be issued in the following cases:

  • Errors in billing or duplicate charges.
  • Membership cancellations due to medical conditions (documentation required).
  • Termination of services by Inferno Commit to Be Fit Gym for reasons unrelated to a member's actions.

1.3. Trial Memberships:

Fees for trial memberships or introductory offers are non-refundable unless specified otherwise.

2. Class and Program Fees

2.1. Cancellations by Members:

  • Single Classes: Refunds for missed single classes are not provided.
  • Prepaid Packages or Programs: Refunds for prepaid packages or programs may be considered if cancellation occurs at least 10 days before the start date.

2.2. Cancellations by the Gym:

If a class or program is canceled by Inferno Commit to Be Fit Gym, members will receive a full refund or the option to reschedule.

3. Personal Training Sessions

3.1. Non-Refundable Fees:

Payments for personal training sessions are non-refundable.

3.2. Rescheduling:

Members may reschedule a session without penalty if at least 24 hours’ notice is provided. Sessions canceled with less than 24 hours’ notice will be forfeited.

4. Retail Purchases

4.1. Returnable Items:

Products purchased at our gym may be returned within [X days] of purchase, provided they are unopened, unused, and in their original condition.

4.2. Non-Returnable Items:

Certain items, such as gift cards, water bottles, and customized merchandise, are non-returnable and non-refundable.

4.3. Defective Products:

Defective products may be exchanged or refunded upon inspection and approval.

5. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. ubmit a Request: Contact our customer service team at [Email Address] or [Phone Number]. Provide your full name, membership details, and a description of the issue.
  2. Documentation: Include relevant documentation, such as a receipt, proof of payment, or medical certificate if applicable.
  3. Processing Time: Refund requests will be reviewed within [7-14 business days]. Approved refunds will be issued using the original payment method.

6. Changes to This Policy

Inferno Commit to Be Fit Gym reserves the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on our website, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
