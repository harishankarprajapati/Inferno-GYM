Inferno Commit to Be Fit Gym we values your privacy and is committed to providing a transparent experience regarding how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help us improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and providing relevant information.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: To enable website functionality, such as account login and secure payment processing.
  • Performance Cookies: To collect information about how visitors use our site, such as pages viewed and error messages, so we can improve performance.
  • Functional Cookies: To remember your preferences, such as language or region settings, for a more personalized experience.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: To deliver tailored advertisements based on your browsing behavior and preferences.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

First-Party Cookies

These cookies are set by our website and are primarily used to ensure the site functions properly.

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services, such as analytics and advertising platforms, which may place cookies on your device. These cookies are subject to the policies of the respective third-party providers.

4. Your Choices

Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies entirely or notify you before a cookie is stored. However, disabling cookies may impact the functionality of our website.

Opting Out of Targeted Advertising

To opt out of targeted advertising cookies, you can use the following tools:

  • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out Tool
  • Google Ads Settings

Do Not Track Signals

Our website does not currently respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals from browsers.
